## Engineering Philosophy

> *"Gamified platforms are excellent for first-contact syntax acquisition, but engineering depth requires building something that breaks."*

This portfolio demonstrates a deliberate progression from syntax-level learning to production-grade systems engineering. The strategic pivot from entry-level platforms to university-level coursework and hands-on capstone projects reflects a mature engineering approach focused on:

- **Memory-aware systems thinking** — understanding what happens at the hardware level
- **Type-safe design patterns** — `enum class`, template metaprogramming, bitmask permissions
- **State machine architecture** — ATM PIN FSM, calculator undo system, bank menu dispatch
- **Production-grade systems** — multi-layered banking architecture with RAII, permission control, and transaction safety
